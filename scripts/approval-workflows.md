# Sample Approval Workflows

## PR under Rs 25,000

1. Employee submits PR.
2. Reporting Manager approves, rejects or sends back with mandatory comments.
3. Finance checks budget, duplicate request, cashflow impact and vendor dues.
4. Finance approves and PO can be created.

## PR Rs 1,00,001 to Rs 5,00,000

1. Employee submits PR.
2. Reporting Manager approves.
3. Finance Head approves after budget and liability review.
4. Finance creates PO and emails vendor.

## PR above Rs 5,00,000

1. Employee submits PR.
2. Reporting Manager approves.
3. Finance Head approves.
4. Founder/CEO approves total commitment and cashflow impact.
5. Finance creates PO.

## Invoice Processing

1. Vendor submits invoice against PO.
2. System checks duplicate invoice, PO amount overrun, missing GST and receipt status.
3. If receipt is incomplete, invoice is held.
4. Once receipt is complete, Finance schedules payment.
5. Finance Head or Founder approves payment release where required.
